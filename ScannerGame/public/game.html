You are a level 1 scientist tasked with scanning a new creature found on an alien planet. Keep your scanner, the magnifier 3000x, on the creature as much as possible so that the computer can gather data on the creature. The amount of data gathered will be displayed in the upper right hand corner. Data decays slowly when the scanner is not on the creature. Try to gather as much data as possible, you only have a few minutes. Before you work with an actual creature we will have a practice run with a somewhat tame creature. After the game is over you will be asked a series of questions about your experience. Then you will be taken to a debriefing screen where you will be thanked and debriefed. Do not click back or refresh the page during the game or your progress will be lost.


When you are ready, click the rectangle.

You are done with practice. On the next screen will be a much more active specimen that we need scanned. The IDs and scores of our best scientists will be the top left corner of your screen.
Please do your best.

When you are ready to begin, click the rectangle.
